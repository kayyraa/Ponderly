• Ponderly Follow System and Its Algorithm Ponderly's follow system prioritizes posts based on user interactions. When a user follows another, the followed user's posts are consistently displayed at the top of the page, ensuring greater visibility. • Ponderly Vote System and Its Algorithm Ponderly's voting system is designed to prioritize original content, ensuring that the creators of authentic posts receive greater visibility. By ranking original posts higher in user feeds, the system prevents low-effort or unauthorized reposts from overshadowing the original author's work. This approach encourages genuine engagement, rewards creativity, and maintains the integrity of the platform’s content ecosystem. • Ponderly Comment System Ponderly's comment system serves as the primary space for users to express their thoughts, share feedback, and engage in meaningful discussions. Whether users want to provide constructive criticism, voice their appreciation, or engage in debates, the comment section allows for open interaction within the community. This system fosters engagement by enabling users to react, respond, and contribute to conversations in a way that enhances the overall discussion experience on the platform.
